About Us

Travel With Us is a student travelling platform that inspires students to plan their adventures, suggesting affordable experiences

Established in 2019 by former Aston University alumni's, Travel With Us founders had just returned from their travels, slightly jetlagged but on the biggest high of their lives. Inspired by the planet, the people they had met and the places they had seen, they set out on a mission to recommend and review affordable countries and city break excursions and worldly adventures to other students, young people and explorers.
